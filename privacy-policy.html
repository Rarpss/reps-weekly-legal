🔐 Reps Weekly Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 17, 2025

Reps Weekly (“we,” “us,” or “our”) explains how and why we access, collect, store, use, and/or share your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including:

  • Downloading and using our mobile application (Reps Weekly)
  • Using related services linked to this Privacy Notice

Purpose: Reps Weekly helps users log workouts, visualize progress, and stay motivated — individually and through team features.

Questions? Contact us at repsweekly@gmail.com

📌 Summary of Key Points

  • What Data? Voluntarily provided & app interaction data
  • Sensitive Data? No
  • Third Parties? No unsolicited collection
  • Why? Improve services, fulfill obligations
  • Security? Industry-standard measures
  • Your Rights? Varies by region — access, erase, restrict, object

🧾 1. What Information Do We Collect?

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Workout data (reps, types, intensity)
  • Profile settings
  • Team affiliation

Data is stored securely using Firebase, with App Check and anonymous analytics for UX optimization.

🔄 2. How Do We Process Your Information?

  • Enable login and account features
  • Operate and improve app
  • Prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal duties
  • Protect vital interests

⚖️ 3. Legal Bases for Data Processing

EU / UK: Consent, legal obligations, vital interests

Canada: Express/implied consent; legal exceptions (fraud, claims, public info)

4. Sharing Your Personal Information

Shared only if the business merges, transfers, or partners — subject to safeguards.

5. Data Retention

Stored only as long as necessary for legal and operational reasons; otherwise deleted or anonymized.

6. Keeping Your Data Safe

  • Firebase authentication
  • App Check for integrity
  • Limited access protocols

Internet transmission is never 100% secure — use Reps Weekly in trusted environments.

7. Data from Minors

We do not knowingly collect data from users under 18. Discovered accounts will be deactivated and deleted.

8. Privacy Rights

  • Access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction
  • Withdraw consent by contacting us

Submit requests via repsweekly@gmail.com
