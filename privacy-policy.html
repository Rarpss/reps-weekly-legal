Reps Weekly Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 09, 2025

Reps Weekly (“we,” “us,” or “our”) explains how and why we access, collect, store, use, and/or share your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including:

  • Downloading and using our mobile application (Reps Weekly)
  • Using any related apps or services linked to this Privacy Notice
  • Participating in marketing, events, or sales-related activities

Purpose of Reps Weekly: Reps Weekly is a personalized fitness tracking app designed to help users log bodyweight exercises, visualize progress, manage intensity, and stay motivated. It supports consistent training habits and includes team-based features for social engagement.

Questions or Concerns?
If you have any questions, contact us at repsweekly@gmail.com.

Summary of Key Points

  • What Information Do We Process? We process personal data based on your interactions with Reps Weekly, including the features you use and the info you voluntarily provide.
  • Do We Process Sensitive Data? No. We do not process sensitive data like racial or ethnic origin, sexual orientation, or religious beliefs.
  • Do We Collect Data From Third Parties? No, all personal data is collected directly from you.
  • How Do We Use Your Data? We use it to provide and improve the app, secure your account, prevent fraud, and comply with law. We may also use it for other purposes with your consent.
  • Who Might We Share It With? We may share data only in specific situations with trusted third parties. Details provided in the full policy.
  • How Is Your Info Protected? We use industry-standard organizational and technical safeguards. However, no transmission or storage method is 100% secure.
  • What Are Your Rights? Your privacy rights may vary based on your location (e.g., GDPR in EU/UK). You can submit access or deletion requests to exercise these rights.
  • Do We Use Advertising? Yes. We use Google AdMob to serve ads, which may collect device data to improve ad relevance and performance.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information You Provide

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Workout data (reps, exercise types, difficulty selection)
  • Profile settings
  • Team affiliation

Note: All data must be accurate, and updates must be communicated to us.

We rely on secure infrastructure provided by Firebase to manage authentication, data storage, and app integrity features such as App Check.

Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to serve ads within the Reps Weekly app. AdMob may collect device-specific data such as identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID or IDFA), IP address, usage statistics, and location (if enabled) to deliver and optimize ad performance. These ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your device settings and regional regulations. Learn more at Google’s Advertising Privacy & Terms.

We may collect anonymized performance and interaction data using analytics tools to improve user experience. This data does not identify individuals and is used solely for app optimization.

Application Data

  • Push notifications (you may opt out via device settings)
  • Internal analytics and performance metrics

Sensitive Info

We do not collect or process sensitive personal information.

Google API

Use of Google APIs follows the Google API Services User Data Policy, including limited use provisions.

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

  • Enable account creation, login, and management
  • Operate and improve app functionality
  • Communicate with you as needed
  • Prevent fraud and secure the platform
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect individual safety (e.g., vital interest in emergency cases)
  • Serve advertising via Google AdMob, including collecting data to optimize ad delivery and measure performance

Additional processing may occur only with explicit user consent.

3. Legal Bases for Data Processing

If You Are in the EU or UK:
We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal Obligations: For law enforcement or regulatory compliance.
  • Vital Interests: To protect safety or prevent harm.
  • Other legal bases depending on your jurisdiction (see below).

Advertising and Consent:
Advertising-related data processing is conducted on the basis of your consent, which you can manage via your device or platform settings, such as ad personalization controls. This includes mechanisms provided by operating systems (e.g., Android, iOS) or browser-level consent banners. These settings allow you to control how and whether advertising identifiers are used for personalized experiences.

If You Are in Canada:
Processing may occur through:

  • Express or Implied Consent
  • Exceptions under Canadian law, including:
    • When timely consent isn’t feasible
    • Fraud detection
    • Legal claims or subpoenas
    • Witness statements
    • Journalistic or artistic use
    • Publicly available information

We may also disclose anonymized data for approved research or statistical purposes under ethical oversight.

4. When and With Whom Do We Share Your Personal Information?

If our business is sold, merged, or transferred in whole or part, your personal information may be included as part of that transaction in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

5. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Notice
  • Comply with applicable legal requirements (e.g., tax, accounting)

Once we no longer need your data:

  • We will delete or anonymize it
  • If deletion isn’t immediately possible (e.g., due to backups), we isolate the data and protect it until removal is feasible

6. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

We use appropriate technical and organizational safeguards, including:

  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Enforced App Check protocols
  • Limited access to data stores

However, no method is 100% secure. Transmission of data over the internet is done at your own risk, and you should only use Reps Weekly within a secure environment.

7. Do We Collect Information From Minors?

Reps Weekly does not knowingly collect or market data to anyone under the age of 18 (or the equivalent minimum legal age in your region).

If we discover that minor data has been collected:

  • We will deactivate the account
  • We’ll delete personal information promptly

Parents or guardians who believe their child’s data was shared can contact us at repsweekly@gmail.com.

8. What Are Your Privacy Rights?

Depending on your location (e.g., US states, EEA, UK, Switzerland, Canada), your rights may include:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Delete your data
  • Restriction: Limit how we process it
  • Portability: Transfer your data elsewhere
  • Objection: Prevent specific types of processing
  • Human Review: For automated decisions that carry legal significance

You can make these requests via repsweekly@gmail.com and we will respond in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Withdrawing Consent

If we process your data based on consent, you can withdraw that consent anytime by contacting us. This does not affect previous lawful processing.

Account Information

To update or delete your account:

  • Email repsweekly@gmail.com with your request
  • We’ll remove your data from active systems, but may retain limited records to:
    • Prevent fraud

9. Controls for Do-Not-Track Features

Some web browsers and mobile operating systems include a Do-Not-Track (DNT) feature that signals your privacy preference to block tracking of online activities. However:

  • There is no standardized DNT protocol recognized across platforms
  • We do not currently respond to DNT signals or automatic tracking opt-out mechanisms
  • If a universal standard is adopted in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy to reflect it

California DNT Disclosure:
There is no current industry or legal standard for honoring DNT signals, so we do not respond to them at this time.
