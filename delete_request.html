Request Account & Data Deletion

This page also provides an external method for users to request deletion of their Reps Weekly account and associated data.

Quick summary:
  • Email us from the email address linked to your Reps Weekly account if possible.
  • Use the subject line Delete My Account.
  • We’ll confirm your request and delete eligible account data once verified.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email our support team with the subject line: Delete My Account
  2. Include:
    • The email address associated with your Reps Weekly account
    • Optional: your UID from the app, if available
    • Optional: a short note confirming you want your account deleted
  3. Send your request to: repsweekly@gmail.com
Verification: To protect users, we may need to verify account ownership before processing deletion. The fastest method is emailing us from the account’s registered email address. If you no longer have access to that email, we may ask for additional information.

What Gets Deleted

When a valid deletion request is confirmed, we will delete or de-identify account-related data such as:

  • Account profile information associated with authentication
  • Your user identifier and account access records
  • Workout history, including rep counts and timestamps stored against your account
  • Progress tracking and performance metrics stored against your account
  • Account-linked preferences or feature flags stored against your account

What May Be Retained

Some information may be retained only where reasonably necessary, including:

  • Support emails and related communications
  • Security, fraud prevention, and abuse monitoring records
  • Aggregated or de-identified analytics that cannot reasonably identify you
  • Data retained to comply with legal obligations

Timeframe

Target deletion timeframe: We aim to complete deletion within 7 days after confirming the request. In some cases, such as where verification is required, it may take longer.
