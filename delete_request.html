Request Account & Data Deletion

This page is provided by Reps Weekly to help users request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Deletion

Email our support team with the subject line:

Delete My Account

Include the following in your message:

  • The email address associated with your Reps Weekly account
  • (Optional) Your UID from the app (visible in Profile screen)

Send your request to: repsweekly@gmail.com

What Gets Deleted

  • Account credentials (e.g., email, authentication tokens)
  • Workout history including rep counts, timestamps, and session data
  • User ID (UID) and any device-linked identifiers
  • Progress tracking and performance metrics
  • Any cached in-app preferences or feature toggles tied to the account

Retention: Once your request is confirmed, all personal and workout data will be permanently deleted within 7 days. No data is kept beyond this period unless required by law (e.g., anonymized crash logs or fraud audit trails).
